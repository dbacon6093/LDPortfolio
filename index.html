Featured Project

Scenario-based simulation for internal tech support

An AI-Driven Simulation in Articulate Storyline

This interactive scenario is an AI-powered scenario-based simulation where learners interact with a frustrated internal stakeholder, investigate a CRM workflow issue, and choose an appropriate next step. Learners practice de-escalation, active listening, and root-cause questioning while receiving an immediate score and targeted feedback based on the quality of their response.

This project focuses on

Building practical communication and diagnostic skills through an AI-powered workplace simulation. Learners practice de-escalating a frustrated stakeholder, asking targeted questions, identifying the root cause of a CRM workflow issue, and deciding whether to troubleshoot, clarify the process, or escalate the concern.

My role included

Designing the scenario structure, writing the AI character prompts, developing realistic learner goals, and creating feedback criteria to evaluate learner responses. I built the interaction around authentic workplace communication skills, including de-escalation, active listening, root-cause questioning, and selecting an appropriate support or escalation path.

About Me

I spent 19 years designing and leading learning experiences in public education, building repeatable systems that helped learners develop skills, respond to feedback, and improve performance over time. That work shaped my approach to instructional design: learning should be practical, realistic, and built around what people actually need to do.

My current portfolio focuses on scenario-based learning, leadership development, AI-supported simulations, and performance support resources. I’m especially interested in designing scalable learning systems that help employees communicate clearly, adopt new processes and tools, and navigate challenging workplace situations with confidence.
