Featured Project

Navigating Difficult Conversations

Scenario-based e-learning experience built in Articulate Storyline

This interactive scenario helps learners practice managing a difficult workplace conversation where expectations, communication, and performance have become misaligned. Instead of presenting advice alone, the experience allows learners to make decisions, see consequences, and adjust their approach in a realistic setting.

This project focuses on

Clarifying expectations during performance conversations, maintaining trust while addressing problems directly, recognizing how tone and assumptions shape outcomes, and choosing responses that move conversations forward productively.

My role included

Scenario design, dialogue writing, branching structure development, learner decision mapping, feedback design, and creation of supporting reference materials.

Launch Project View Design Approach

Why This Experience Was Designed This Way

Difficult conversations often break down not because people lack good intentions, but because expectations are unclear and communication happens too late. I designed this scenario to let learners explore how small choices in tone, timing, and framing can change the direction of a conversation.

Rather than presenting a single correct path, the experience includes multiple realistic responses with different outcomes. Learners can recover from missteps, see how defensiveness develops, and practice strategies that rebuild alignment.

The goal was to create something that feels less like a quiz and more like a conversation they might actually have at work.
